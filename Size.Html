How to Measure Yourself

For the most accurate sizing, take these measurements while wearing form-fitting clothing.

Chest/Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape measure horizontal.

Waist

Measure around the narrowest part of your natural waist, usually just above the belly button.

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, about 8 inches below your waist.

Inseam

Measure from the crotch to the bottom of your ankle along the inside of your leg.
