Accessibility Statement

Our Commitment

At Cartify, we believe in creating an inclusive shopping experience for all our customers. We are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Our goal is to make Cartify.com perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust for all users, regardless of ability.

Accessibility Guidelines

Cartify strives to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities, and user-friendly for everyone.

Perceivable

Provide text alternatives for non-text content, create content that can be presented in different ways, and make it easier for users to see and hear content.

Operable

Make all functionality available from a keyboard, give users enough time to read and use content, and help users navigate and find content.

Understandable

Make text readable and understandable, make content appear and operate in predictable ways, and help users avoid and correct mistakes.

Robust

Maximize compatibility with current and future user tools, including assistive technologies.

Accessibility Features

Keyboard Navigation

Our website can be navigated using only a keyboard. Use Tab to move forward, Shift+Tab to move backward, and Enter to activate elements.

Text Resizing

Use the text size controls at the top of the page to increase or decrease text size for better readability.

High Contrast Mode

Toggle high contrast mode for better visibility if you have low vision or sensitivity to bright colors.

Alternative Text

All meaningful images on our site include alternative text descriptions for screen reader users.

Clear Structure

We use proper heading structure and semantic HTML to ensure our content is well-organized and easy to navigate.

We Value Your Feedback

We are continuously working to improve the accessibility of our website. If you encounter any barriers while using Cartify.com, or have suggestions for how we might improve accessibility, please contact us.
