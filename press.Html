Company Information

About Cartify

Cartify is India's fastest-growing e-commerce platform, connecting millions of buyers and sellers across the country. Founded in 2018, we've revolutionized online shopping with our customer-first approach and innovative technology.

Our Mission

To make online shopping accessible, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone in India and beyond.

Our Vision

To become the world's most customer-centric e-commerce platform, where people can find and discover anything they want to buy online.

Company History

Cartify was founded in 2015 by Shubham Kumar, who identified a gap in the market for an e-commerce platform that truly understood the needs of Indian consumers. Starting with just 10 employees and 100 sellers, we've grown to become one of India's leading e-commerce platforms with over 1,200 employees and 85,000+ sellers.

Key Milestones

  • 2018: Company founded with initial seed funding
  • 2019: Reached 1 million registered users
  • 2020: Expanded to 50 cities across India
  • 2021: Launched mobile app with AI recommendations
  • 2022: Achieved 125% YoY growth in Q4
  • 2023: Raised $50M in Series C funding
