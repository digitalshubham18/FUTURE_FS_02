Cancellation Policy

At Cartify, we understand that sometimes you may need to cancel an order. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both our customers and our business.

Orders Eligible for Cancellation

You can cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it, or before it has been shipped. Once an order has been shipped, it can no longer be canceled but may be eligible for return.

Processing Time

Cancellation requests are processed within 4 business hours. You will receive an email confirmation once your cancellation is complete.

Refund Timeline

Refunds are processed immediately upon cancellation confirmation. It may take 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your payment method and bank.

Non-Cancellable Items

Some items such as personalized products, digital downloads, and perishable goods cannot be canceled once ordered. These exceptions are clearly marked on product pages.
