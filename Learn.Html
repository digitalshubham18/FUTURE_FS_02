Our Story

From Small Beginning to Fashion Destination

Cartify was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to make fashion accessible to everyone. What started as a small boutique has grown into a premier online destination for men's and women's fashion.

Our journey began when our founder, Sarah Johnson, noticed a gap in the market for high-quality, affordable fashion that could be accessed from anywhere. She started with just a handful of products and a passion for style.

Today, we offer thousands of products from hundreds of brands, serving customers across the country. Despite our growth, we've stayed true to our original values of quality, affordability, and exceptional customer service.

Our Mission & Vision

At Cartify, our mission is to empower individuals to express themselves through fashion. We believe that what you wear is an extension of who you are, and everyone deserves to feel confident and stylish.

Our vision is to become the most customer-centric fashion brand in the world, where people can find everything they need to express their personal style.

We're committed to sustainability and ethical practices. As we grow, we're working to reduce our environmental impact and ensure fair working conditions throughout our supply chain.

Why Choose Cartify

Free Shipping on All Orders

We offer free shipping on all orders over $50, with no hidden fees or charges. Our efficient logistics network ensures your order arrives quickly and in perfect condition.

For orders under $50, we charge a flat rate of $4.99 for shipping. We also offer express shipping options for those who need their items sooner.

All orders are processed within 24 hours and shipped from our distribution centers located across the country to minimize delivery times.

Hassle-Free Returns

Not happy with your purchase? We offer a 30-day return policy for all items in their original condition. Returns are free and easy.

Simply initiate a return through your account page, print the prepaid shipping label, and drop off the package at any shipping location.

Once we receive your return, we'll process your refund within 3-5 business days. For exchanges, we'll ship your new item immediately.

Secure Payment Options

Your security is our priority. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal and payment information.

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are processed securely through trusted payment gateways.

Your payment information is never stored on our servers, ensuring complete security and peace of mind with every purchase.

24/7 Customer Support

Our customer support team is available around the clock to assist you with any questions or concerns.

Reach us via live chat, email, or phone. Our average response time is under 5 minutes for live chat and within 2 hours for email inquiries.

We also have an extensive FAQ section and helpful buying guides to assist you in making the right purchase decisions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. Express shipping options are available at checkout for delivery within 1-2 business days. International shipping times vary by destination.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all items in their original condition with tags attached. Returns are free for US customers. Simply initiate a return through your account page and use the prepaid shipping label provided.

How do I know what size to order?

We provide detailed size charts for each product to help you find the perfect fit. You can also check customer reviews for insights on sizing. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Please note that customers are responsible for any customs duties or import taxes that may apply.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also view your order status and tracking information by logging into your account and visiting the order history page.
