Returns & Exchanges

We make returns and exchanges easy. If you're not completely satisfied, we're here to help!

Our Return Policy

At Cartify, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a hassle-free return and exchange policy within 30 days of delivery.

30-Day Window

Return or exchange items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or store credit.

Free Returns

We provide free return shipping for all eligible items in the US.

Easy Process

Start your return online and drop off your package at any authorized shipping location.

How to Return an Item

Follow these simple steps to return or exchange an item:

1

Start Online

Initiate your return through our online portal

2

Pack Item

Pack the item in its original packaging

3

Print Label

Print the prepaid shipping label we provide

4

Ship Back

Drop off at any authorized shipping location

Once we receive your return, we'll process your refund within 3-5 business days. The refund will be issued to your original payment method.

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your item must be in the same condition that you received it, unworn or unused, with tags, and in its original packaging. You'll also need the receipt or proof of purchase.

Items that cannot be returned:

  • Gift cards
  • Downloadable software products
  • Some health and personal care items
  • Personalized or custom-made products
  • Items marked as final sale

Please get in touch if you have questions or concerns about your specific item.

Exchanges

We gladly accept exchanges for items in new condition within 30 days of delivery. The fastest way to obtain an exchange is to return the item you have, and once the return is accepted, make a separate purchase for the new item.

If you need a different size or color, please follow the return process and place a new order for the item you want.

Refunds

We will notify you once we've received and inspected your return, and let you know if the refund was approved or not. If approved, you'll be automatically refunded on your original payment method within 10 business days.

Late or missing refunds:

If you haven't received a refund yet, first check your bank account again. Then contact your credit card company, it may take some time before your refund is officially posted. Next contact your bank. There is often some processing time before a refund is posted. If you've done all of this and you still have not received your refund yet, please contact us at support@Cartify.com.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process a return?

Once we receive your return, it typically takes 3-5 business days to process. After processing, your refund will be issued to your original payment method. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your bank or credit card company.

Do I have to pay for return shipping?

No, we provide free return shipping for all eligible items. Once you initiate your return, we'll email you a prepaid shipping label that you can use to return your item at no cost to you.

What if my item is damaged or defective?

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our customer service team immediately at support@Cartify.com or call us at 1-800-CARTIFY. We'll arrange for a free return and send you a replacement as soon as possible.

Can I return sale items?

Yes, sale items can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a refund or exchange. The only exceptions are items marked as "final sale" which cannot be returned.

What if I received the wrong item?

If you received the wrong item, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of delivery. We'll provide a prepaid shipping label for you to return the incorrect item and expedite shipment of the correct item to you.
