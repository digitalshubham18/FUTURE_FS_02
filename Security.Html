Account Security Tips

Use Strong Passwords

Create unique, complex passwords for your Cartify account. Use a combination of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. Avoid using easily guessable information like birthdays or common words.

Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Add an extra layer of security to your account by enabling two-factor authentication. This requires both your password and a verification code sent to your phone when signing in from new devices.

Enable 2FA

Watch for Phishing Attempts

Be cautious of emails or messages claiming to be from Cartify that ask for personal information. We will never ask for your password or payment details via email. Always check the sender's address and look for signs of suspicious activity.

Regularly Review Account Activity

Periodically check your account activity and order history for any unfamiliar actions. Report any suspicious activity to our security team immediately.

Security Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if I suspect unauthorized access to my account?

If you suspect unauthorized access to your account, immediately change your password and enable two-factor authentication if you haven't already. Review your recent account activity and contact our security team for assistance. We also recommend checking your payment methods for any unauthorized transactions.

How can I recognize phishing emails pretending to be from Cartify?

Legitimate Cartify emails will always come from an @Cartify.com domain. Be wary of emails that create a sense of urgency, contain spelling or grammatical errors, or ask for sensitive information like passwords or payment details. When in doubt, navigate directly to Cartify.com instead of clicking links in emails.

Is it safe to use public Wi-Fi when shopping on Cartify?

While our website uses encryption to protect your data, we recommend avoiding making purchases on public Wi-Fi networks. If you must use public Wi-Fi, consider using a VPN for an added layer of security. For sensitive transactions, use a trusted private network.

How does Cartify protect my payment information?

We use tokenization and encryption to secure your payment information. Your full credit card details are never stored on our servers. For added security, we comply with PCI DSS standards and regularly undergo security audits to ensure your payment data remains protected.
