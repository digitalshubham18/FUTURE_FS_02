Cookies Policy

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, serve personalized content, and analyze our traffic. By clicking "Accept All", you consent to our use of cookies.

About Cookies

This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage them. This policy is part of our commitment to ensuring that your privacy is protected while using our website.

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, shopping cart, and account management.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to track visitors across websites to display relevant advertisements and personalize content.
