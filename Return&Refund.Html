Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process a refund?
Once we receive your returned item, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. It may take additional time for your bank to process the refund.
Do I have to pay for return shipping?
We provide free return shipping for items that are defective or incorrect. For other returns, a shipping fee may be deducted from your refund.
What if I received a damaged item?
Please contact us immediately at support@Cartify.com with your order number and photos of the damaged item. We'll send a replacement right away.
Can I exchange an item instead of returning?
Yes, you can request an exchange for a different size or color if available. Select the exchange option when initiating your return.
