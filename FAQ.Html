Frequently Asked Questions

Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. Express shipping options are available at checkout for 1-2 business day delivery.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping times vary by destination but typically take 7-14 business days. Additional customs fees may apply.
How can I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the Order History section.
What are your shipping rates?
We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50. For orders under $50, standard shipping is $4.99. Express shipping options start at $9.99.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for most items in original condition with tags attached. Some items like clearance products and intimate apparel may have different return conditions.
How do I return an item?
You can initiate a return through your account dashboard or by contacting customer service. We'll provide a prepaid return label and instructions for returning your item.
How long does it take to process a refund?
Once we receive your returned item, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment method and financial institution.
Do you offer exchanges?
Yes, we offer exchanges for size and color if available. You can request an exchange through the returns process in your account or by contacting customer service.

Payments & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Cartify Gift Cards.
Is my payment information secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your payment information. We do not store your full credit card details on our servers.
Why was I charged more than the listed price?
If you're seeing a different amount on your statement than the listed price, it could be due to sales tax (which varies by location) or international transaction fees from your bank for orders outside the US.
Do you offer price matching?
We offer price matching within 7 days of purchase if you find the same item at a lower price on our website or at an authorized retailer. Contact our customer service team for assistance.
