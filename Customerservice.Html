Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your Cartify account and visiting the "Order History" section. Alternatively, you can use the tracking link provided in your shipping confirmation email. If you're having trouble locating your order, please contact our support team.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items in original condition. Some products may have different return timelines or conditions (e.g., final sale items). To initiate a return, please visit the Returns Center in your account or contact our support team for assistance.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to create a new password. If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, please check your spam folder or contact our support team.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. You can see the shipping options and costs during checkout before completing your purchase. Some restrictions may apply to certain products or destinations.

How can I update my payment method?

You can update your payment method by logging into your account, going to "Payment Settings," and selecting "Add Payment Method." You can also set a default payment method for future purchases. If you encounter any issues, our support team can assist you.
