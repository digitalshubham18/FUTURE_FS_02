Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery is available for an additional fee and takes 1-2 business days. Delivery times may vary during holiday seasons or due to unforeseen circumstances.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items in their original condition. Some items like electronics have a 14-day return window. Items must be unused and in original packaging with all tags attached.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the 'Order History' section. You'll also receive a tracking number via email once your order ships, which you can use on the carrier's website.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Cartify gift cards. We also offer installment payment options through select providers.

How do I reset my password?

Click on 'Forgot Password' on the login page and enter your email address. We'll send you a link to reset your password. If you don't receive the email within 10 minutes, check your spam folder or contact our support team.
