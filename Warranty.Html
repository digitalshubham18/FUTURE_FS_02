Warranty Coverage Details

What's Covered

Your Cartify product warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use for a specified period from the date of purchase. This includes:

  • Manufacturing defects
  • Hardware failures
  • Performance issues not caused by misuse
  • Defective components

What's Not Covered

The warranty does not cover:

  • Damage caused by accident, abuse, or misuse
  • Cosmetic damage not affecting functionality
  • Problems caused by using unauthorized accessories
  • Damage from liquid contact or exposure to extreme environments
  • Normal wear and tear

Warranty Period

The standard warranty period varies by product category:

  • Electronics: 1 year
  • Appliances: 2 years
  • Furniture: 5 years
  • Accessories: 6 months

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check my product's warranty status?

You can check your warranty status by entering your product's serial number on our warranty check page. Alternatively, you can contact our support team with your proof of purchase.

How do I make a warranty claim?

To make a warranty claim, please visit our warranty claims portal and provide your product details and proof of purchase. Our support team will guide you through the process and arrange for repair or replacement if eligible.

What should I do if my product is out of warranty?

For products out of warranty, we still offer repair services for a fee. Contact our support team to get a quote for out-of-warranty repairs. We also often have trade-in programs for older products.

Does the warranty cover international purchases?

Cartify warranties are valid internationally for products purchased from authorized retailers. However, service options may vary by country. Please contact our international support team for region-specific information.
