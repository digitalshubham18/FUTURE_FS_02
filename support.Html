Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your Cartify account and visiting the "Order History" section. Alternatively, you can use the tracking link provided in your shipping confirmation email. If you're having trouble locating your order, please contact our support team.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items in original condition. Some products may have different return timelines or conditions (e.g., final sale items). To initiate a return, please visit the Returns Center in your account or contact our support team for assistance.

How can I change my shipping address?

You can update your shipping address in the "Account Settings" section of your Cartify account. If your order has already been shipped, we may not be able to change the delivery address. Please contact our support team immediately for assistance with orders that have already been placed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Cartify Gift Cards. Some payment options may not be available in all regions.

How do I create a Cartify account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right of any page on our website. You'll need to provide your email address, create a password, and verify your email. Once your account is created, you can save your shipping addresses, payment methods, and track your orders more easily.
